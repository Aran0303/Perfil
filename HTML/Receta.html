Cordelia's Recipe for Resurrection

Ingredients

For the brownies:

  • 2 cups of white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups of flour
  • 1 cup of melted butter
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup of cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • A cup of walnut halves

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. Grease a pan with baking spray.
  3. Whisk sugar, flour, melted butter, eggs, cocoa powder, vanilla, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl until combined.
  4. Spread the batter into the prepared pan
  5. Decorate with walnut halves.
  6. Bake into the preheated oven until top is crinkled and a toothpick comes out wiht a few moist crumbs, about 20 to 30 minutes.
